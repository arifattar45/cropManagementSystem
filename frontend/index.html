Frequently Asked Questions

🌾 Which crops does PlantDoc support?

PlantDoc supports major crops like wheat, rice, maize, pulses, and many fruits & vegetables. More crops are continuously added.

🔍 How accurate are the results?

Our AI model is trained on thousands of crop images with over 90% accuracy. Better image quality improves prediction confidence.

🧴 Are the treatment suggestions safe?

Yes, all suggestions are based on trusted agricultural practices. Always follow recommended dosages for fertilizers or pesticides.

🌐 Can I use PlantDoc in my local language?

Yes! PlantDoc supports multiple languages. More local language options will be added over time.

📱 Do I need an internet connection to use PlantDoc?

Yes, an internet connection is required to process images and get real-time AI predictions and treatment suggestions.
